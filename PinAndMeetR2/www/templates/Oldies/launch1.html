Facebook Account

We highly recommend using a Facebook account to sign in. This brings you the following benefits.

Security. Facebook provides high security for all the personal information

Fast. No extra registration form is needed to be filled

No extra steps. No email confirmation and profile picture upload needed

Safe. Pin'n'Meet will never post anything on your wall. Facebook is used only for authentication. Facebook won't provide any personal information without first asking your permission. For more information see Facebook Developer site.

By signing in you agree to the terms of use.

Pin'n'Meet Account

If you don't like to use Facebook, you can always create a Pin'n'Meet account. A valid email address is required for email varification.
